Spaghetti

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe is simple, delicious, and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned.
  3. Add garlic to the skillet and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the marinara sauce and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Drain the spaghetti and toss it with the sauce.
  6. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top.
