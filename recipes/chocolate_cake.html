Chocolate Cake

Description

This rich and moist chocolate cake is the perfect dessert for any chocolate lover.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin).
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans and cool completely on wire racks.
