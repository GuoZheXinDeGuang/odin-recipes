Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is a hearty, cheesy, and delicious meal perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned.
  4. Spread a layer of marinara sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Layer noodles, ricotta cheese, ground beef, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers, finishing with a top layer of mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
